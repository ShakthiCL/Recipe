Fired Rice

    Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste


Cooking Instructions.

Step 1:Place green onions, ginger, and garlic in a food processor; puree until smooth.
Step 2:Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season chicken thighs with salt and cook, skin-side down, until browned, about 10 minutes per side.
Step 3:Scrape ginger puree into the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, 2 to 3 minutes. Add curry powder, red curry paste, garam masala, and Chinese five-spice powder; cook and stir until fragrant.
Step 4:Return chicken thighs to the skillet, reduce heat, and simmer until flavors are combined, about 20 minutes.



Red Curry

    Ingredients

  • 6 green onions, white parts only, chopped
  • 1 (2 inch) piece peeled fresh ginger
  • 4 cloves peeled garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce


Cooking Instructions.

Step 1:Place green onions, ginger, and garlic in a food processor; puree until smooth.
Step 2:Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season chicken thighs with salt and cook, skin-side down, until browned, about 10 minutes per side.
Step 3:Scrape ginger puree into the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, 2 to 3 minutes. Add curry powder, red curry paste, garam masala, and Chinese five-spice powder; cook and stir until fragrant.
Step 4:Return chicken thighs to the skillet, reduce heat, and simmer until flavors are combined, about 20 minutes.
